How It Started

From one tired parent's spreadsheet to a platform families trust.

Our founder, a former pediatric nurse and new mom, couldn't find a single resource that explained what her daughter's development actually meant. So she built one — first for herself, then for the parent group down the street.

  • Founded by clinicians, not marketers
  • Shaped by thousands of parent conversations
  • Independently reviewed by child psychologists
